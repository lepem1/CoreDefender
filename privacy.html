Your data is used only to:

Data is stored securely and retained only for as long as necessary to provide verification and security services. Data may be deleted periodically or upon request, unless retention is required for security or moderation reasons.

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties. Data may only be disclosed if required by law or legal process.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. User Rights

You may stop using the service at any time. You may request access to or deletion of your data, subject to security and moderation requirements.

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the service indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
